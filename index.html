    • How to join ?

    Buy some cardano on one exchange platform. Kraken.com for instance.

    Create a wallet. With Yoroi on your smartphone, or Daedalus on your laptop.

    Make multiples copies of the many words you were given. These are the keys of your wallet.

    At this point, you should find a bundle of adresses in your "receive" tab. Copy the first one.

    Go to the exchange and perform a withdraw, providing the amount of cardano you want to transfer (don't be shy :-)) and paste the address.

    After a while you should see your updated wallet balance (the amount you withdrawed minus the transaction fee ~1 ADA)

    Once you did this, you go to the delegate Tab, and delegate your wallet to this stake pool (aka WILDR, remember : WILDR like the page title here)

    In order to do this you will need to deposit 2 ADA, 2 ADA you will get back if you ever decide to retire from stacking.

    And because registering to a stake pool is a transaction, you will need to pay a transaction fee, ~ 1 ADA.

    You might wonder what I'm going to do with you wallet ? Nothing. I do not own your wallet, you just told the system that your wallet was not dedicated to trading but to stacking, and that you were in my team that's all :-).

    Whatever you decide to do, you are always the owner of your funds.
